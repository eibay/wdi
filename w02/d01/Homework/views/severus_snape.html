Severus Snape

Professor Severus Snape was a half-blood wizard who was the son of the witch Eileen Snape and Muggle Tobias Snape. During his lifetime, Severus Snape was Potions Master, Defence Against the Dark Arts professor, and Headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry (which he attended as a student from 1971 until 1978). He was also member of the Order of the Phoenix and played a very important role in both of the Wizarding Wars against Lord Voldemort.
