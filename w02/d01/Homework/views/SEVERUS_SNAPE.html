Severus Snape

Cunning, Resourcefulness, and Ambition

Professor Severus Snape (9 January, 1960) is the son of the witch Eileen Snape (née Prince) and Tobias Snape. During his lifetime, Severus Snape was Potions Master (1981–1996), Defence Against the Dark Arts professor (1996-1997), and Headmaster (1997-1998) of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry (which he attended as a student from 1971 until 1978)
