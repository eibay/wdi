Slytherin Students

Slytherins tend to be ambitious, shrewd, cunning, strong leaders, and achievement-oriented. They also have highly developed senses of self-preservation.[9] This means that Slytherins tend to hesitate before acting, so as to weigh all possible outcomes before deciding exactly what should be done.

Pansy Parkinson

Pansy Parkinson was a witch in Harry Potter's year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Pike

He was part of Draco Malfoy's gang in 1993 and 1994 and was one of the Slytherins that imitated a Dementor in a effort to scare Harry in the first Care of Magical Creatures class of the year.
